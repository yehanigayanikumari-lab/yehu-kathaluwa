The Artist

A passion painted into every canvas

Yehu Kathaluwa is a Sri Lankan artist whose work spans the full spectrum of painting — from the rich texture of oil and acrylic to the luminous wash of watercolour and the precision of digital art.

What makes Yehu's work truly distinctive is the tradition of saree painting — a centuries-old craft brought into a contemporary voice, turning silk into a living canvas worn and celebrated.

Based in Sri Lanka, Yehu takes commissions for portraits, landscapes, abstract works, and bespoke painted sarees for weddings and special occasions.

The Gallery

A Wearable Canvas

Painted Sarees

Hand-painted on silk

Yehu's saree paintings are one-of-a-kind pieces created for brides, bridesmaids, and special occasions. Each saree is hand-painted with motifs drawn from nature, tradition, or personal story.

Popular designs include floral fields, peacocks, lotus motifs, and custom portraits. Every saree is treated with care so the paint bonds permanently with the silk.

Available in cotton, georgette, and pure silk. Custom orders welcome — the design is yours to choose.
